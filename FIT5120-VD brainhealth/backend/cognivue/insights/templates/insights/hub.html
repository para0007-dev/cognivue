Vitamin D & Brain Health

Low vitamin D has been associated with smaller brain volumes and poorer cognitive scores in observational research. Sun exposure, skin type, season, clothing coverage, and diet/supplements all influence your vitamin D status.

What factors matter?

  • Sun exposure: time outdoors between 10am–3pm
  • Skin type: darker skin synthesises vitamin D more slowly
  • Location & season: southern states in winter have lower UV
  • Clothing coverage: more covered = less UV to skin
  • Diet & supplements: oily fish, fortified milk/margarine, eggs; supplements can help
