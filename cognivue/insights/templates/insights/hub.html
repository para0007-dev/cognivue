Why Vitamin D Matters for Your Brain
  • Maintains hippocampus size and structure crucial for memory.
  • Up to 17% lower dementia risk with adequate levels.
  • Supports cognitive function and processing speed.
  • Prevents age-related decline and executive dysfunction.
